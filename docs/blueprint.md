# **App Name**: MedCoderX Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display the MedCoderX name, subtext, and a brief welcome message. Includes prominent call-to-action buttons to the Google Play Store.
- App Showcase: Showcase two apps, 'SafeAllergy' and 'PhotoRights AI', with app icons, names, concise feature lists, and direct links to Google Play Store.
- PhotoRights AI - Copyright Checker Tool: AI-powered suggestions for checking image copyright status
- About Me Section: Introduce MedCoderX with a short bio highlighting passion for building simple and useful apps.
- Contact Section: Provide a contact email and a simple form (optional) for user inquiries.
- Footer: Include essential links such as About, Contact, Terms of Use, and Privacy Policy, along with a copyright notice.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) for a modern and premium feel.
- Background color: Light grey (#F5F5F5) to maintain a clean, minimal aesthetic.
- Accent color: Neon blue (#1E90FF) for buttons and interactive elements to add a futuristic touch.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a clean and readable appearance throughout the site.
- Use Google Play Store icons for app store links; app icons in rounded squares.
- Card-based app showcase layout to highlight app features. Responsive design for optimal viewing on all devices.
- Soft fade-in effects on scroll to enhance user experience. Subtle hover effects on cards and buttons.
